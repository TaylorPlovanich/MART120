Story Summary

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko.

Nezuko has become a demon herself.

Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.

Tanjiro joins a secret society, known as the Demon Slayer Corps, that has been waging a war against demons for centuries.
